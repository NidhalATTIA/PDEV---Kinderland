What is "Kinderland" ?


The aim of this project is to create a social network to solve this problem and to facilitate the daily life of parents as well as kindergartens. Institutes will be able to create profiles for their kindergartens. Therefore, parents can easily consult and register their children. They can also leave feedback and a rating for each institute.

As you know, the digital world is essential today in everyday life. Many parents have a lot to manage and do not have the time to find a suitable kindergarten for their children and keep track of their routes properly Parents are looking to find a trustworthy kindergarten. How to get the opinion of other parents and how to know if it is a good daycare? How to be able to register their children quickly and remotely without wasting time? Our role is to find a solution to all these problems and provide the best experience for parents.
